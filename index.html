Welcome to Low RAMP

Low RAMP is a MATLAB Low rank matrix factorization solver based on Belief-Propagation, following the equations in http://arxiv.org/abs/1503.00338 (Presented in ISIT 2015) and http://arxiv.org/abs/1507.03857 (Presented in Allerton 2015). It follows a series of work on approximate message passing.

Comments and remarks regarding bugs or functionalities are more than welcome, participation is also appreciated.
